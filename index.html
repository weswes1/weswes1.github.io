Game Section

Asteroid Frenzy

** ONLY playable on desktop or laptop. No mobile or tablet support yet! Controls: use the four arrow keys to move the black ball. The goal of this game is avoid getting too large, if you hit anything besides the blue ball, your size will increase. Try to stay alive as long as possible to get a hiscore!

SATURN ESCAPE

Controls: Use the up key to keep the star ship flying in the correct area. Avoid colliding with the top and bottom wall or the asteroids! The goal of this game is to stay alive as long as possible. Try to get a hiscore!

PIRATE INVASION

Controls: Use the right arrow button to charge up the cannon and hit the invading pirate ship.

RAT RACE

Controls: Use the arrow keys, G key, and space bar to move the ball and change its color. If the color of the ball and the bar are mismatched, the game ends. Try to stay alive as long as you can!
