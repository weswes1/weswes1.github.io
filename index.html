Asteroid Frenzy

** ONLY playable on desktop or laptop. No mobile or tablet support yet! Controls: use the four arrow keys to move the black ball. Controls: Use the up key to keep the star ship flying in the correct area. Avoid colliding with the top and bottom wall or the asteroids! The goal of this game is to stay alive as long as possible. Try to get a hiscore!
